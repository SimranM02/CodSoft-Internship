About Me

Hi! My name is Simran Makwane. I'm a fourth-year Computer Science student at Amity University Noida, deeply passionate about the intersection of technology and creativity. Beyond coding and algorithms, my diverse set of hobbies adds vibrant dimensions to my life.

As an avid debater, I thrive on the thrill of intellectual discourse and honing my persuasive skills. Representing my country in international debate competitions, I've embraced the art of effective communication. Off the debating stage, I find solace in the rhythmic expressions of Bharatanatyam, a classical dance form that has been an integral part of my life for a decade.

In the digital realm, I channel my creativity through digital drawing and graphic design. Whether sketching on a digital canvas or crafting visuals on platforms like Canva and Photoshop, I love bringing ideas to life through visual storytelling.

As I navigate the final year of my computer science journey, my commitment to both the analytical and creative aspects of the field remains unwavering. With a fervor for technology and a palette of diverse interests, I'm poised to embark on a dynamic professional journey that seamlessly blends innovation and creativity.
